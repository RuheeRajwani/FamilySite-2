Laila

It is very hard to explain what a mother is. It is a very passionate feeling being a person who loves, takes care and nurtures unconditionally. As my kids are growing up who are my heart and soul, who walk around with dreams and aspirations for their future twinkling in their eyes. Want to see my family happy, healthy and prosperous, mostly being humble and generous with their talents in helping people in times of need. I guess, it sums up my identity as Laila Rajwani, who loves to travel, surrounded with peace and laughter.
